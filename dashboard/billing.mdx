Billing controls whether the active workspace can use FiveMesh services.

The dashboard treats active and trialing subscriptions as service access. When a workspace does not have active access, CDN and Cache setup remain locked.

## What to check

Open **Billing** from the dashboard sidebar to review the current workspace subscription state.

Check billing when:

- A service says an active subscription is required.
- A checkout or plan change just completed.
- You switched from personal workspace to an organization.
- You expected an organization to share services but the service is still locked.

## Access states

| State | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| Active or trialing | Continue to service setup. |
| No active subscription | Pick a plan or complete checkout. |
| Billing needs attention | Update the subscription before access changes. |
| Organization has no shared subscription | Ask the owner to activate billing or switch back to your personal workspace. |

## After checkout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Return to the dashboard">
    After checkout, return to **Billing** or **Overview**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Refresh the workspace state">
    If the dashboard has not updated yet, refresh the page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the service">
    Open **CDN** or **Cache** from the sidebar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The docs do not list plan limits because limits can change. Use the pricing page and dashboard billing state as the source of truth.
</Note>
